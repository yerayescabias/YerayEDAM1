About us

In January 2024, I founded the neurodegenerative diseases association in Eibar to provide comprehensive support to patients, caregivers, and the community. We focus on offering emotional support programs, education, and medical collaborations to enhance the quality of life for those affected. Our goal is to be a symbol of hope and solidarity in the fight against these diseases.
