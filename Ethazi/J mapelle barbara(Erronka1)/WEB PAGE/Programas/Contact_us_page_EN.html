about us


We are a new small company in Euskal Herria about Do It Yourself garage, the company started in 2016 and in 2018 we get our first boxes and we made our unique brand of products. Now we are making it bigger for to provide those tools to other provinces.

Here you'll find the best selection of boxes, tools and products to repair , modify ... your car, if you have doubts, we have the best crew of mechanics in whole Basque Country whith the best formation to solve you every single problem.

We have some facilities to make your visit a great and satisfying experience. You will be able to find three different buildings: Small Boxes, Big Boxes, and Offices.

Small Boxes:

The purpose of these boxes is to repair small and medium vehicles, such as motorcycles or medium-sized cars. If you have this type of vehicle, it will be enough for you!

Big Boxes:

These are designed for vehicles of considerable or large size. They are perfect for repairing trucks or large vehicles, like vans.
